Scratch

1. Go to the Scratch Website and sign into your account.

2. Open a new project.

3. In order to give the sprite (characters) motion like dancing or walking, click on the blue button, "Motion", that is on the left hand side of the screen.

    3a. Choose a motion that you would like your sprite to do.

    3b. NOTE: Make sure that the motion for the sprite is all around. Not just horizontal or vertical.

4. In order to give a "new appearance" and make the sprite learn something, you will need to click on the purple button, "Looks", that is on the left hand side of the screen.

    4a. In order for the sprite to think a thought or say something you will need to use the say and think blocks.

    4b. You can also change the appearance of the sprite by switching costumes and choosing color effects.

    4c. You can also change the backgrounds from the project, and you can apply the effects all together.

5. If you want your sprite to complete a certain action after a certain condition, you can use event and control.

    5a. You can have your sprite start their action after the green flag is clicked, a certain key is pressed, or after a certain amount of time, you can use the blocks in these categories.

    5b. If you want something to be repeated then you can use control blocks.
