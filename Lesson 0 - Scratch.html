Scratch

1. Go to the Scratch Website.

2. Click the Join Scratch button.

3. Choosing a Username and Password (If under the age of 13, ask for parent permission to get a Scratch Account)

    3a. Choose a Username (Keep it something simple to remember.)

    3b. Choose a Password (Don't make it too easy to GUESS, but YOU DO HAVE TO REMEMBER IT!)

    3c. Re-Enter Your Password

    3d. Go Ahead and Click Next!

4. Enter Personal Information (If under the age of 13, ask for parent permission.)

    4a. Enter your Birth Month and Year

    4b. Specify your Gender

    4c. Enter the Country you are CURRENTLY living in.

5. Confirming your Account Part I (If under the age of 13, ask for parent permission.)

    5a. Enter your Email

    5b. Re-Enter your Email

    5c. OPTIONAL: Click "Receive updates from the Scratch Team"

6. Confirming your Account Part II (If under the age of 13, ask for parent permission.)

    6a. Log into your email.

    6b. Confirm your Scratch Account
