Our Background

We want to teach and give the opportunities of learning computer science to everyone. Computer science is not just people sitting down at a computer with thick glasses on, but it is an experience which contributes to our society. It is all about innovative thinking. You don’t need to know how to code to learn computer science, it is how to solve problems with your ingenious abilities. We are all interested in computer science. Additionally, we want to help other students to have success and improve their skills, like ours.

Focus on What You Do Best

You can pick up on any lesson at any point of time. Then, when you come back and sign in you can resume your lesson. This is an ongoing “course”.
